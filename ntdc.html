NSTL-DRDO National Technology Day Celebration (NTDC)-2024

Subject: Invitation for National Technology Day Celebration (NTDC)-2024

Date: 08 May 2024

Venue: NSTL-DRDO, Visakhapatnam

NTDC-2024 is being celebrated at NSTL-DRDO on 10th May 2024. The event includes open house exhibitions, multimedia presentations of NSTL-DRDO technologies, and exhibits by various institutes on the theme "From Schools to Startup: Igniting young Minds to Innovate." Selected participants are requested to report to Ramnath Secondary School, NSTL DRDO Residential complex for registration.
